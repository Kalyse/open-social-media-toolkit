Public barred from Gateway Announcement | NowPublic News Coverage

Via Grist on Dec 12, 2008

Posted by 1 hour ago

Vote it up 62Post a comment 12Share it

Residents of Surrey and Vancouver were blocked from entering the site of an announcement regarding the controversial Gateway Program on Monday.The announcement was held on private property behind barbed wire fences. Some in attendance speculated this was a deliberate attempt to keep the public from attending an event about the project which is becoming increasingly unpopular.
